                                The Chinese considered 30 to be a number of cunning and strategy.
                              Rooted in the ancient text The Thirty - Stratagems, it symbolizes 
                             the wisdom of knowing when to strike, and when to retreat. In this game, 
                            30 isn’t just a number — it’s your target, your challenge, your battlefield.
                          Draw your cards wisely. Outsmart the odds. Can you reach 36 without going over?

Rules:

    
  • Achieve a total of exactly 30 to win the game.
    • 
    
  • Exceeding a total of 30 results in an automatic loss.
    • 
    
  • If your total remains below 30, you may continue drawing cards in hopes of a 30.
